Objective

Create a robot capable of estimating its position and driving toward target points using encoder feedback and sensor-based heading information.

My Contribution

  • Explored PID tuning and sensor filtering for smoother control.
  • Logged and analyzed motion data using MATLAB/Python plots.

Hardware

  • ESP32 microcontroller
  • 12 V encoded DC motors
  • BTS7960 motor drivers
  • MPU6050 IMU
  • 12 V battery and buck converter
